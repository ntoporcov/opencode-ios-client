OpenClient is a native iPhone client for connecting to your own OpenCode server.

Keep your coding sessions within reach when you are away from your desk. Browse projects, resume chats, answer permission and question prompts, and follow ongoing activity from a fast native interface built for iPhone.

OpenClient includes:

- Connect to your own self-hosted OpenCode server
- Browse projects and sessions
- Continue chats from a native mobile UI
- Respond to permission and question prompts
- Follow active work with Live Activities
- Reconnect quickly to saved servers

OpenClient is designed for private, self-hosted use. You choose which server it connects to, and your session data stays tied to your own OpenCode setup.
